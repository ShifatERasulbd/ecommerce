The Philosophy of Void

Elena Vostokova
Editor in Chief
October 24, 2023

Minimalism isn't about having less. It's about finding the essence of things, the silence between the noise. In this exploration of modern living, we look at the concept of "negative space"—not as emptiness, but as a powerful tool for clarity and focus.

From the architecture of our stores to the layout of our homes, every element serves a purpose. The void allows the object to breathe, to exist without apology.

"Perfection is achieved, not when there is nothing left to add, but when there is nothing left to take away."

Embracing the void in design

When designing our new collection, we started with a blank canvas. We asked ourselves: "What does this product need?" Not "What can we add?" By stripping away the non-essential, we arrived at the core of functionality.

This philosophy translates beautifully to our blog, the clothing we choose, and the way we present our brand. It’s about confidence. It’s about letting the product speak for itself without being drowned in unnecessary detail.

Join us as we deconstruct the elements of modern minimalism in fashion and lifestyle, and discover how you can apply these principles to your own life.
